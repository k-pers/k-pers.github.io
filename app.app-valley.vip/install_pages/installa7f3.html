Drum Pad
The wireless (or wired) velocity sensitive midi drum controller for your iPhone or iPad.

Play your mac's midi drum and percussion instruments, with velocity sensitive expression, and minimal latency using midi over wifi or with super low latency in wired mode. The variable pad layout lets you display 1 to 6 pads on screen at once.

Presets
Pad name, midi and channel numbers can be configured per pad and saved as presets to allow quick switching between sources.

Ultra low latency
For ultra low latency and stability just plug into your mac using a standard USB charging cable and launch our free VMidi Server application on your mac. No midi interface is required and device can charge while playing!
