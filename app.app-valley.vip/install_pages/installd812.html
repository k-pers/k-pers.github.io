Mirrored lets you create stunning images by mirroring a part of a photo in one single touch.

See the app in action at https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AUH23Rk-oPA

Select a photo, or take a new one, select one of the 8 mirror effects, and start having fun!
Just touch the point where you want to mirror your photo and see in real time the mirrored image !


Mirrored offers 3 modes and 8 different and unique mirror effects.
Modes:
- Scaled modes: the mirrored part of your photo is scaled to fill the new image. These modes create some fun deformations!
- Original mode: the mirrored part is as the original photo part. This mode allows you to keep the proportions of your photos.
Mirror effects:
-HL: Horizontal Left, the left part of the photo is mirrored horizontally
-HR: Horizontal Right, the right part of the photo is mirrored horizontally
-VT: Vertical Top, the top part of the photo is mirrored vertically
-VB: Vertical Bottom, the bottom part of the photo is mirrored vertically
-Q1: 1st Quarter, the top left part of the photo is mirrored horizontally and vertically
-Q2: 2nd Quarter, the top right part of the photo is mirrored horizontally and vertically
-Q3: 3rd Quarter, the bottom right part of the photo is mirrored horizontally and vertically
-Q4: 4th Quarter, the bottom left part of the photo is mirrored horizontally and vertically

The Q1, Q2, Q3, and Q4 effects are perfect to create beautiful kaleidoscope photos. Horizontal mirror effects are great to check a face symmetry and create funny portrait of your friends and family.

Save your mirrored photos in your photo library, or export them to other apps (Facebook, Mail?).
